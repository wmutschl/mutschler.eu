Willi Mutschler

On leave. From April 2026 through March 2027, I am on leave from the University of Tübingen to work for DSGE-net on Dynare.

I am an assistant professor at the University of Tübingen, where I work on international macroeconomics and computational methods.

My research focuses on geoeconomics, the macroeconomic effects of war, and rare-disaster modeling, alongside work on DSGE models and macroeconometrics. It has been published in the American Economic Review, the Journal of Economic Dynamics and Control, Economic Modelling, Econometrics and Statistics, and Wirtschaftsdienst.

My teaching covers macroeconomics and econometrics, including the popular courses Computational Macroeconomics and Quantitative Macroeconomics, which are publicly available on GitHub.

As a member of the core developer team of Dynare, I maintain the identification and method-of-moments toolboxes and offer teaching materials and video tutorials.

You can reach me at willi@mutschler.eu.

Find me elsewhere: GitHub, ResearchGate, Ideas, Google Scholar, YouTube.
